A bigger vision

More than a ranch.

At Bee Creek Farm and Aroma of Grace, the vision is bigger than a ranch. It is a place of restoration — where faith, healing, land, animals, prayer, sensory peace, and human connection come back together the way they were always meant to.

Built from years of counseling experience, trauma-informed care, equine-assisted learning, faith-based mentoring, and a deep calling toward restoration, Bee Creek Farm is being designed as a peaceful refuge for people who are weary, burned out, grieving, overwhelmed, traumatized, disconnected, or simply longing to breathe again.

This is not "escape from life." This is returning to what matters.

What healing looks like here

Simple and sacred.

  • Quiet mornings.
  • Horses that regulate nervous systems without speaking a word.
  • Donkeys that make people laugh again.
  • Prayer that grounds the heart.
  • Essential oils and sensory rituals that help the body exhale.
  • Fresh sourdough and warm cinnamon rolls shared around a table.
  • Hands in soil.
  • Sunsets over Texas pastureland.
  • Restoration through rhythm, relationship, and presence.

The foundation

Mind · Body · Spirit.

The foundation of the work is Mind · Body · Spirit restoration — integrating faith, neuroscience-informed care, equine connection, sensory regulation, and practical healing rhythms in a safe and grounded environment.

The mission is simple: to help people stabilize, reconnect, rebuild, and rediscover purpose.

Mind

Neuroscience-informed care, trauma awareness, and the steady work of getting a tired brain back to safety.

Body

Equine connection, sensory rituals, essential oils, fresh bread, and the practical rhythms that help a body exhale.

Spirit

Prayer, faith-rooted reflection, time under wide Texas skies, and the quiet return to who you were made to be.

And then there are the donkeys

Rocky & Harry.

Rocky

The mini spotted donkey

Big personality, comic relief, and pure greeter energy. The first one to the fence, the loudest hello on the property.

Harry

The steady full-size donkey

Calm, grounding, and quietly present. He doesn't need to do much — he just is, and somehow that's enough.

Together, they represent so much of the heart behind Bee Creek Farm: strength and gentleness, peace and playfulness, rescue and restoration.

Aroma of Grace
